Tennis Wearable + App

As part of my MSc dissertation, I designed and built a working prototype tennis wearable and companion React Native app that captured and visualised key biomechanical metrics of the serve, such as wrist pronation and swing speed. The project was completed end-to-end by me, from hardware and software development to UX design and user testing, which validated both the technical accuracy and usability of the final product. The project acts as a proof of concept with further development possible.
