Menos

People have become more conscious of what they put on and in their bodies. Gender barriers are being dismantled, and people are increasingly becoming aware of the climate and environmental issues that face us today. These three insights led to the creation of Menos, meaning less in Portuguese and Spanish. This brand believes that less is more, creating gender-neutral, sustainable shampoo products that are good for you and the environment.
